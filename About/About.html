Scope

SAStools.org was made with the intention to guide users to the best tools for analyzing small-angle scattering data. I have co-developed some tools and this website hosts links to those.
There are many other fantastic programs out there (see, e.g. smallangle.org), but this site just display those I am co-developing.

With the hope that it is useful for you.
